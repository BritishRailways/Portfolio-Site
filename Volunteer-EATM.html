East Anglia Transport Museum

I have been volunteering at the East Anglia Transport Museum since OOctober 2023.

Since then, I have gained valuable experience in museum operations and maintenance.

I have filled many roles and spent many hours contributing to the museum's operations.

At age 15, I joined the museum as a trolleybus conductor where I have spent countless hours riding around on buses collecting tickets and assisting passengers.

Now i'm older, i've taken on more responsibilities and continue to contribute to the museum's operations.

I am now also a tram conductor and work in the maintenance department, fixing various issues with the museum's vehicles and equipment.

Currently, I am training to become a tram driver and hope to take on this role in the near future.
