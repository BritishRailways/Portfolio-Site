Meshtastic

I have been exploring Meshtastic, a project that enables decentralized radio communication using existing radio hardware.

It's a low-power, long-range communication solution that allows devices to communicate directly without relying on traditional infrastructure.

Meshtastic is designed to be easy to use and accessible, making it a great option for outdoor enthusiasts, emergency responders, and anyone interested in exploring the possibilities of radio communication.

I'm excited to continue learning about Meshtastic and its applications, and I look forward to experimenting with it in the future.

Me and a friend have both been working on our own Meshtastic nodes, investigating how it works and its limitations as use of communication in urban and rural environments with varying conditions and terrains.

Baofeng Radio

I have been exploring the Baofeng radio, a popular handheld radio used for amateur radio communication.
