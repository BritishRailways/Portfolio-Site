PA-PI Project

Along with a friend, we competed in the Raspberry Pi competition. It was an exciting experience that allowed us to showcase our skills and creativity.

We designed a project that we could both work on and develop.

We created a prototype of our product which would be a classroom monitor to make classrooms safer for everyone.

We had to create a presentation and a video to submit our project, which was a great opportunity to practice our communication skills and share our work with others.

Overall, it was a rewarding experience that allowed us to learn and grow while working on a project we were passionate about.

What our project was about

Our project focused on creating a smart classroom monitoring system using a Raspberry Pi computer. The goal was to develop a solution that could make a learning environment safe

Key ideas behind our project included:

  • Real-time monitoring of classroom CO2 and temperature levels
  • Automated window opening and closing based on environmental conditions
  • Automatic switching of heaters and air conditioning systems to maintain the best conditions for students

Project Outcomes

Our project was successful in demonstrating the potential of using Raspberry Pi for smart classroom monitoring. We were able to create a functional prototype that addressed the key challenges of maintaining a safe and comfortable learning environment.

It was a great experience to work on this project and learn more about the capabilities of the Raspberry Pi.
